# Hotel Sunrise Internal Policies & Offers

## Overview
Hotel Sunrise is a luxury resort focused on family vacations and corporate retreats. Below are our current guidelines and hidden promotional offers. Our staff should refer to these when accommodating specific guest requests.

## General Information
- **Check-in Time:** 3:00 PM
- **Check-out Time:** 11:00 AM (Late check-out available for $50 up to 2:00 PM)
- **Pet Policy:** We allow up to two dogs per room (under 40 lbs) for a flat $100 non-refundable fee. Cats are not permitted.
- **Breakfast:** Complimentary continental breakfast is served daily from 6:30 AM to 10:00 AM in the Sunset Lounge.

## Current Promotions & Discounts (March 2026 - August 2026)

### 1. The "Family Getaway" Package
- **Details:** If guests book for 4 nights or more, they receive the 5th night completely free.
- **Discount Code (For Staff Reference):** FAM5FREE
- **Eligibility:** Families booking at least one standard suite. Not applicable during major holiday blackout dates (July 3-6).

### 2. The "Business Retreat" Discount
- **Details:** Corporate groups booking 10 or more rooms automatically receive a 15% discount on the total room rate.
- **Perk:** Includes free daily booking of the Main Conference Hall for up to 4 hours.
- **Discount Code:** CORP15

### 3. Local Resident Discount
- **Details:** Residents of the same state with valid local ID receive a 10% discount on all spa services and hotel dining. This does not apply to room rates.

## Frequently Asked Questions (Internal)
Q: Do we offer airport shuttles?
A: No, we currently do not have a dedicated shuttle, but we partner with "Sunrise Rides" who offer a $25 flat rate for our guests if they mention our hotel.

Q: Is the pool heated?
A: Yes, our indoor pool is kept at 82°F year-round. The outdoor pool is currently closed for the winter season and opens on May 1st.
